For more than 25 years, the photographer Bernhard Edmaier, who initially studied geology, has been dedicated to photographing the surface of our planet - the forces of Nature which form the Earth. His seemingly abstract aerial images, in which high quality photography and science unite, have caused international sensation and have been received with great excitement by international audiences.
